About Me

Hi, I'm Alexandra Mitchell. Three years ago, I moved to Dubai for a job opportunity, and I had no idea that I'd develop what my friends now call an "obsession" with swimming pools. But here we are.

I'm not a travel blogger, an influencer, or someone who gets paid to write reviews. I work in marketing for a tech company, and I started visiting pools around the UAE as a way to unwind after long work weeks. What started as weekend relaxation turned into something more—a genuine interest in understanding what makes a pool experience truly exceptional.

Living in Dubai, I quickly realized that pool culture here is different from anywhere else I've lived. Pools aren't just places to swim—they're social spaces, relaxation destinations, and sometimes, works of art. The attention to design, service, and atmosphere varies dramatically from place to place, and I found myself taking mental notes every time I visited a new pool.

Why I Started This Site

After about a year of exploring pools across Dubai and Abu Dhabi, friends started asking for recommendations. "Where should we go for a special occasion?" "Which pool is best for families?" "Where can we actually swim, not just lounge?"

I realized that most pool reviews online are either marketing copy from hotels or brief mentions in travel blogs. There wasn't a resource that gave honest, detailed, firsthand experiences from someone who's actually been there multiple times, paid their own way, and taken notes.

So I decided to create one. This site is my personal collection of experiences, written honestly and in detail. Every pool I review has been visited at least twice, during different times of day and year. I pay for my own access, I take notes, and I write about what I actually experience—the good, the bad, and the details that make each place unique.

My Review Process

I don't have a formal rating system, but I do have a consistent approach:

  • Multiple visits: I visit each pool at least twice, ideally at different times of day and different seasons. A pool that's magical at sunset might be too hot at midday. A pool that's peaceful in the morning might be too busy in the afternoon.
  • Personal experience: I pay for my own access. I don't accept comped visits or sponsored reviews. This keeps my perspective honest and unbiased.
  • Detailed notes: I take notes on everything—water temperature, service quality, atmosphere, food, amenities, crowd, and those intangible details that make an experience memorable.
  • Honest writing: I write about what I actually experience. If something is expensive, I say so. If service is exceptional, I describe why. If a pool has flaws, I mention them.

What You'll Find Here

This site focuses on pools that stand out for one reason or another. I'm not trying to review every pool in the UAE—that would be impossible and not particularly useful. Instead, I'm documenting the pools that offer something special: exceptional views, unique features, outstanding service, or that indescribable quality that makes an experience memorable.

Each review includes:

  • First impressions and overall atmosphere
  • Detailed descriptions of the pool experience
  • Service and amenities
  • Crowd and atmosphere
  • What makes each pool special
  • Practical information for visitors
  • Honest final thoughts

My Perspective

I'm someone who appreciates good design, values service quality, and believes that details matter. I'm not looking for the cheapest option—I'm looking for experiences that justify their cost through quality, atmosphere, and attention to detail.

I'm also someone who believes in honest reviews. If a pool is expensive, I'll say so. If service is exceptional, I'll describe why. If something doesn't live up to expectations, I'll mention it. My goal isn't to sell you on visiting these pools—it's to give you enough information to decide if they're right for you.

What's Next

I'm constantly exploring new pools and revisiting favorites. This site will continue to grow as I discover new places worth writing about. I'm particularly interested in finding pools that offer something unique—whether that's a stunning view, innovative design, exceptional service, or an atmosphere that can't be replicated.

If you have suggestions for pools I should visit, or if you want to share your own experiences, feel free to get in touch. I'm always interested in hearing about pools I haven't discovered yet.

Thanks for reading, and I hope these reviews help you find your perfect pool experience in the UAE.

— Alexandra Mitchell
