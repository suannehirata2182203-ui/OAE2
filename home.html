My Personal Journey Through UAE's Most Stunning Pools

After three years living in Dubai and exploring pools across the Emirates, I've compiled honest, firsthand reviews of the pools that truly stand out. No marketing fluff—just real experiences from someone who's actually been there.

From infinity pools overlooking the Arabian Gulf to resort pools that feel like private oases, here's my personal ranking based on atmosphere, service, and that indescribable feeling of pure relaxation.

Why These Reviews Matter

I'm not a travel blogger or influencer. I'm someone who moved to Dubai for work and fell in love with the pool culture here. These reviews come from multiple visits, different seasons, and honest observations about what makes each pool special—or not.

Every pool on this list has been visited at least twice, during different times of day and year. I've paid for my own access, waited in lines, experienced the service, and taken notes on everything from water temperature to the quality of the poolside food.
